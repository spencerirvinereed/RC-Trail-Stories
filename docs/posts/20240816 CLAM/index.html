17 Months in LA

LA
Author

DK

Published

August 16, 2024

Left home on 18 January 1983

I was endowed on 7 January 1983 in Washington DC Temple.

Left for LA on 16 February. 1983

Left winter in Utah and LA seemed like a paradise

President Harold1 and Virginia2 Hoopes greeted me.

My trainer, Matthew Hunt met me and drove me through Beverly Hills.

I had high hopes but when we arrived at 3250 Country Club Drive, I was disappointed. This area had its heyday, a long time ago.

My second mission president was Clarence Campbell3 (Elna Shakespeare)

I stayed in the Olympic Ward my entire mission I also served in the Cerritos Korean Branch (17909 Bloomfield Ave, Cerritos, CA 90703)

I have stayed in these apartments.

  • 3250 Country Club Drive
  • 4826 Melrose Avenue
  • 16812 Ardmore Avenue

  • 200 Mariposa Avenue
